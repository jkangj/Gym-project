Pulldown Tutorial

Step 1: Starting Position

Grip the bar slightly wider than shoulder-width, with your arms fully extended overhead. Sit upright with your chest up and your feet flat on the floor.

Arm Angle: Begin with an arm angle of approximately 160 degrees between your shoulder, elbow, and wrist.

Step 2: Pull the Bar Down

Pull the bar down towards your chest in a controlled motion while keeping your elbows pointed downward. Focus on contracting your back muscles.

Target Arm Angle: Aim to bring the angle between your shoulder, elbow, and wrist to approximately 45 degrees.

Step 3: Return to Starting Position

Slowly let the bar return to the starting position, fully extending your arms while maintaining control.

Return to Starting Arm Angle: Allow your arms to extend back to approximately 160 degrees.

Step 4: Complete the Repetition

Each repetition should take at least 1 second to complete. Avoid using momentum to pull the bar and focus on controlled movement.
