Squat Tutorial

Warning: Ensure proper form when squatting to avoid injury. Maintain a manageable weight and use a spotter if necessary.

Step 1: Starting Position

Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, toes slightly pointed outward, and a barbell or dumbbells positioned securely.

Starting Angle: Keep your hips, knees, and ankles aligned with an angle of approximately 160 degrees.

Step 2: Lower into the Squat

Slowly lower your body by bending your knees and pushing your hips back. Keep your chest up and your back straight.

Target Angle: Lower until the angle between your hip, knee, and ankle is approximately 90 degrees.

Step 3: Return to Standing Position

Press through your heels to return to the starting position. Keep the movement controlled and avoid locking your knees at the top.

Return to Starting Angle: Return to an angle of approximately 160 degrees.

Step 4: Complete the Repetition

Each repetition should take at least 2 seconds to complete. Focus on form and control to maximize effectiveness.
