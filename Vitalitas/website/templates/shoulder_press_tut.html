Shoulder Press Tutorial

Step 1: Starting Position

Stand upright or sit on a bench with back support. Hold a dumbbell in each hand at shoulder height with your palms facing forward.

Arm Angle: Begin with your arms bent at approximately 45 degrees between your shoulder, elbow, and wrist.

Step 2: Press the Dumbbells Up

Push the dumbbells upward until your arms are fully extended above your head. Avoid locking your elbows to maintain tension.

Target Arm Angle: Extend your arms to an angle of approximately 160 degrees.

Step 3: Lower the Dumbbells

Slowly lower the dumbbells back to the starting position while keeping control of the weights.

Return to Starting Arm Angle: Lower until your arms return to approximately 45 degrees.

Step 4: Complete the Repetition

Each repetition should take at least 1 second to complete. Focus on form and controlled movements for maximum effectiveness.
