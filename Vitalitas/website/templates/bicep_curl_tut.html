Bicep Curl Tutorial

Step 1: Starting Position

Stand upright with a dumbbell in each hand and your arms fully extended. Your palms should be facing forward. Ensure your elbows are close to your torso.

Arm Angle: Aim for a starting angle of approximately 160 degrees between your shoulder, elbow, and wrist.

Step 2: Curl the Dumbbells

Slowly curl the dumbbells while keeping your upper arms stationary. Focus on contracting your biceps as you lift the weights.

Target Arm Angle: Bring the angle between your shoulder, elbow, and wrist to approximately 30 degrees.

Step 3: Lower the Dumbbells

Gradually lower the dumbbells back to the starting position. Avoid letting the weights drop too quickly to maintain control.

Return to Starting Arm Angle: Allow the angle to return to approximately 160 degrees.

Step 4: Complete the Repetition

Make sure each repetition takes at least 1 second to complete to maximize effectiveness. Keep your movements slow and controlled.
